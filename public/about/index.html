Our Mission

Join us as we celebrate 10 years of learning!



Since being founded in 2010, in association with McGill’s Electrical, Computer and Software Engineering Student Society, Code.Jam() has become the largest engineering hackathon at McGill. With over 500 participants, we strive to provide an environment in which students can learn and grow as the next generation of engineers. Code.Jam() offers an opportunity for its participants to apply their knowledge and experience to create innovative and effective solutions to real-life problems in various fields. They then have the opportunity to present their results to a set of industry leading judges and receive helpful and constructive feedback from experts in the field.


Coding

Build an awesome project with your team over the course of the weekend (and get feedback and mentoring throughout).

Problem-Solving

Team up to brainstorm solutions to the problem at hand and apply ideas to come up with effective results.

Discover the Engineering World

Whether it be business insurance, investments, performing arts, or artificial intelligence, the hackathon exposes students to the engineering world.

Network

Meet like-minded hackers and share your knowledge.

Internship Opportunities

Connect with companies looking to hire the next generation of talented developers.
